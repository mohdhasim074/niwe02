Scope

NIWE carries out Inspection Services as per the requirements of ISO/IEC 17020:2012 as an Independent Inspection body (Type A) and to demonstrate the effective implementation of the system at NIWE.

The scope of the Inspection Body is to carry out the Inspection of Wind Turbines and their Components. The detailed scope is defined below:

  1. IAF Scope 18
    2. Machinery and Equipment
      1.1 Tower, Hub and Nacelle Assembly, Rotor Blades
  2. IAF Scope 19a
    3. Electrical equipment
      2.1 Generators, Power Transformers, and Power Converter, and Wind Turbines installed at the Site
